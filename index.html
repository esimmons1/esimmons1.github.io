Hi, I'm Ellis Simmons

With an Associate's degree in Information Technology, I've built a foundational understanding of IT principles, which I complement with my passion for creating things that are both functional and visually appealing. Whether I'm developing interactive graphics, crafting backend logic, or capturing compelling moments through a lens, I enjoy creating things that are both functional and visually appealing. My goal is to further develop these skills as I pursue Computer Engineering.

Beyond my technical pursuits, I enjoy staying active and exploring creative outlets. I am often running, working out, playing ultimate frisbee, where I appreciate the strategic teamwork and athletic challenge, or playing volleyball as a middle blocker or opposite hitter. I'm also an FPV drone pilot, currently studying for my Part 107 certification, which allows me to combine my love for technology with aerial cinematography, capturing unique perspectives of the world.

In my free time, I enjoy watching anime, with favorites like Initial D, Haikyuu, and Beyblade. I often find ways to integrate my interest in anime and physics into my programming projects, such as the map inspired by Initial D or my Black Hole program, which I created after watching an episode of Neil deGrasse Tyson's Startalk. These projects help me explore complex concepts while indulging my creative side.

I got the inspiration for the map from animejs.com, very cool website.
